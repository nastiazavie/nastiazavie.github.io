short biography

From early childhood, a love for music as an art appeared, and in 2001 my parents saw my desire for musical creativity and enrolled in 2 classes of piano and violin, In 2003, she began writing simple melodies, learning to play 2 musical instruments.

In 2010 she successfully completed 7 classes of school. In the same year she entered the Dnieper Academy of Arts. M. Glinka in violin. At the academy, she began to actively engage in the creation of musical works of various kinds (a piece for cello and piano "Life Page 1.").

In three years, in collaboration with the best teachers of the Dnieper Academy of Music, she was optional in the composition among which "Page of Life." Also this year she went to the All-Ukrainian Composers Competition and took 2nd place there. In 2014 she graduated from the Academy of Music. The following 2 years she worked as a violin teacher at a children's music school.

During his work at the music school, the goal appeared to engage in composition, having studied it at the level of the best institution in Ukraine in composition. Therefore, she was actively preparing for admission to Kharkov National University named after Kotlyarevsky. In 2016, she entered the Kotlyarevsky KHNUI for the first year in composition, in the class of People's Artist of Ukraine, V.M. Ptushkin.

In the first year, she took second place in the All-Ukrainian competition of composers with a work written based on the novel of the same name by P. Kulish "Chorna Rada." After that, a number of concerts followed. Also in the first year she took part in an international competition organized by the British educational institution. With the song Love Song,.

At the end of the first year of the first half of the 2nd year, she was passionate about vocal music, so she wrote a vocal cycle and a number of romances. In the second year, she took third place at the All-Ukrainian competition named after Karabitsa In the second year, Diptych was written for a symphony orchestra.
